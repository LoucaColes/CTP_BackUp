CTP Local Controls

Local Player 1
Movement - WASD/Left analogue stick
Camera Movement - Mouse/Right analogue stick
Fire - F/Right trigger
Reload - R/X button
Jump - Space/A button

Local Player 2
Movement - Arrow keys/Left analogue stick
Camera Movement - IJKL/Right analogue stick
Fire - / or Right trigger
Reload - M/X button
Jump - N/A button
